Here you will find the current collection of the prints made from Jennifer's original paintings.

Most of Jennifer's artworks are composed of meticulously painted portraits of hip-hop artists that have left their mark on the music industry. The influence of hip hop culture is universal. People from around the world find common ground in 808 beats, melodic drums and memorable ad-libs. The feeling evoked from listening to this creatively crafted genre of music is commonly expressed through singing song lyrics aloud or dancing, but Jennifer has taken the appreciation and adoration for hip-hop music to the next medium: canvas.

The canvas portraits of these rappers commemorate their longstanding impact on the hip-hop music industry. These limited-edition pieces are presented in A3 sizes, intentionally chosen to best capture the detail and the dimensions of the subject.
